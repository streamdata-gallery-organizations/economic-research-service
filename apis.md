description: The Economic Research Service (ERS) is the main source of economic information
  and research from the U.S. Department of Agriculture and a principal agency of the
  U.S. Federal Statistical System. Located in Washington D.C., the mission of ERS
  is to inform and enhance public and private decision-making on economic and policy
  issues related to agriculture, food, farming, natural resources, and rural development.
  To accomplish this mission, highly trained economists and social scientists develop
  and distribute a broad range of economic and other social science information and
  analysis.
